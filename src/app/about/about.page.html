About
ColdSmart

Heating Ventilation Air-conditioning (HVAC) has the highest share in the electricity load of around 42% in homes and 39% in commercial buildings.

The Philippines may experience a power crisis during 2022 summer months amidst the demand increase with scarce energy reserves; our country having the most expensive power rate in Asia Pacific Region.

RA-11285 Energy Efficiency and Conservation Act of 2019 aims to solve these energy problems.

ColdSmart helps to maintain a clean and sustainable energy to residential (or even commercial) areas especially during peak hot months (from March - August) by simply automating the aircon settings without compromising the comfort level of the user.

ColdSmart also aims to help and as much as possible prevent sudden power interruptions due to high demand of energy supply.

Features of
ColdSmart

Compatible to any worldwide HVAC brand with standard InfraRed remote controls

Smart Schedule - choose your desired day and night temperatures and activates only if premises is occupied. Customize all HVAC comfort settings that you prefer.

Indoor Air Quality (iAQ) Sense - Achieve healthy climate, advises how long to open windows; or warning for risk of developing a mold.

HVAC Zoning - Wireless sensors track which room you are in and auto-adjusts settings.
