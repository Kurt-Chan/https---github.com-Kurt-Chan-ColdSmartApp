Q1:

Is the ColdSmart app free?

ANS:

Yes! it is completely free.

Q2:

Can I add any brand or model of Aircon into the app?

ANS:

Yes, ColdSmart is widely compatible to any Aircon with standard infrared remote controls.

Q3:

Where do I get the Id and Password of the Raspberry Pi?

ANS:

The Id and Password is indicated at the back side of the Raspberry Pi device, or it will be given by the developers.

Q4:

Is internet required in controlling my AC?

ANS:

Yes, ColdSmart is connected to a cloud database that stores every data commands that you set through the app.

Q5:

I can control my aircon through a remote, what makes it different?

ANS:

Through ColdSmart, you can adjust your settings ANYWHERE at ANYTIME as long as you are connected to the internet.

Q6:

What if my phone disconnects to the internet, does it affect the settings that I set?

ANS:

No, the settings that you set will automatically be saved inside the cloud database.

Q7:

How does ColdSmart helps me to manage and save energy?

ANS:

ColdSmart automates your Aircon settings and regulate its usage through the sensors.

Q8:

How does ColdSmart knows where I am inside my house?

ANS:

ColdSmart only uses thermal-motion sensors to determine if there is someone inside a certain area of your house.

Q9:

What is DSM (Demand-Side Management)?

ANS:

It is a program that ColdSmart offers. This includes a contract or agreement between you and the power utility.

Q10:

What are the benefits if I participate to DSM?

ANS:

It ensures a low energy consumption and a yearly rebate per horsepower of air-conditioners

Q11:

What if I don't register to DSM? Can I still use ColdSmart?

ANS:

Definitely yes! DSM is not required in using the app, but we do highly recommend it.

Q12:

If I register to DSM, can they see/know where I am inside my house?

ANS:

No, ColdSmart only uses thermal-motion sensors to monitor if there is someone inside a certain area inside your house.

Q13:

Can I change some settings of my aircon when I am under the DSM Program?

ANS:

No, settings will be unchangeable until the end of the given contract.

Q14:

How do I register to DSM?

ANS:

Visit the Coldsmart website for more info.
